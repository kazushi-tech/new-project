# Japanese Business Document Format

- Use polite form (です・ます調), not honorific (敬語)
- Technical terms: first occurrence as 日本語（English）format
- Tables: Japanese headers, alphanumeric data allowed
- Dates: YYYY年MM月DD日 format
- Numbers: use half-width digits (0-9)
- Bullet points: consistent indentation
